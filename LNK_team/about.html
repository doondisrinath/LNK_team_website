About Us

Preserving the beauty of Indian classical and folk dance traditions through passion, dedication, discipline, and culture.

About Me

I am Vignesh Kartikeya Kandhikattu, a passionate Classical and Folk dancer dedicated to preserving Indian traditional art forms through performance, creativity, and culture.

As a Classical and Folk dancer, my vision through Lalitha Nrutya Kalakshetram is to inspire students with discipline, expressions, rhythm, creativity, and respect for Indian heritage.

I strongly believe dance is not just performance — it is devotion, emotion, storytelling, and tradition combined.

My Guru

My Guru, Dr. Ch. Ajay Kumar Garu, is a renowned Kuchipudi dancer, performer, and choreographer from Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.

He is respected for preserving and promoting the rich traditions of Kuchipudi dance through performances, teaching, and cultural programs.

Under his guidance, I learned expressions, discipline, spirituality, dedication, and respect for culture.

Inspiration Through Dance

The elegance, grace, and dedication of my Guru continue to inspire my artistic journey every single day.

His performances reflect the true spirit of Kuchipudi and the richness of Indian classical dance traditions.

My Father

My father, Sri K. Vasudeva Rao Garu, has always been a pillar of support, encouragement, and motivation throughout my artistic journey.

His belief in discipline, culture, and dedication gave me the confidence to pursue dance with passion and commitment.

His support continues to inspire me to achieve greater heights while staying connected to our traditions.
