The Artist

About Me

I am Vignesh Kartikeya Kandhikattu, a passionate Classical and Folk dancer dedicated to preserving Indian traditional art forms through performance, creativity, and culture.

Through Lalitha Nrutya Kalakshetram, my vision is to inspire students with discipline, expression, rhythm, creativity, and respect for Indian heritage.

I believe dance is not just performance. It is devotion, emotion, storytelling, and tradition brought together through sincere practice.

My Guru

Guided by tradition and the grace of Kuchipudi.

My Guru, Dr. Ch. Ajay Kumar Garu, is a renowned Kuchipudi dancer, performer, and choreographer from Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh.

He is respected for preserving and promoting Kuchipudi through performances, teaching, and cultural programs.

Under his guidance, I learned expression, discipline, spirituality, dedication, and respect for culture.

Support & Strength

My Father

My father, Sri K. Vasudeva Rao Garu, has always been a pillar of support, encouragement, and motivation throughout my artistic journey.

His belief in discipline, culture, and dedication gave me the confidence to pursue dance with passion and commitment.

His support continues to inspire me to achieve greater heights while staying connected to our traditions.
