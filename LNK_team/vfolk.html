About Village Folk

Village Folk dance represents the joyful traditions, celebrations, and lifestyle of rural communities across India.

These energetic dance forms are filled with colorful costumes, expressive movements, and vibrant rhythms.

At Lalitha Nrutya Kalakshetram, students learn authentic village folk traditions with passion, discipline, and cultural pride.
