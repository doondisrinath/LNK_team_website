The academy

Training that blends tradition, technique, and stage confidence.

Lalitha Nrutya Kalakshetram teaches Indian classical and folk dance through steady practice, cultural context, and performance discipline. Students work on posture, talam, expressions, choreography, costume awareness, and group coordination.

The academy is guided by Vignesh Kartikeya Kandhikattu, with a focus on helping children and young dancers grow in confidence without losing the devotional and storytelling roots of the art form.
